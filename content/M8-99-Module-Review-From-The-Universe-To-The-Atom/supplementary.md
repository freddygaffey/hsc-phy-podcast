### Listing 2 — Radioactivity: the three decays and the bookkeeping
| Property | Alpha (α) | Beta (β⁻) | Gamma (γ) |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Nature | Helium-4 nucleus | Fast electron | High-energy photon |
| Charge | +2 | −1 | 0 |
| Ionising power | Highest | Medium | Lowest |
| Penetration | Lowest (paper) | Medium (mm Al) | Highest (cm Pb) |

```text
Inverse rule: high ionising ⇒ low penetration (and vice versa).

DECAY EQUATIONS (conserve mass number A, atomic number Z, and mass-energy):
  Alpha:   A → A−4,  Z → Z−2     (emits ⁴₂He)
  Beta⁻:   A same,   Z → Z+1     n → p + e⁻ + antineutrino   (quark: d → u, weak force / W)
  Beta⁺:   A same,   Z → Z−1     p → n + e⁺ + neutrino       (quark: u → d, weak force / W)
  Gamma:   A same,   Z same      excited nucleus sheds a photon

NEUTRINO: beta particles emerge with a RANGE of energies ⇒ Pauli predicted the
neutrino to carry the missing energy & momentum (same logic Chadwick used → neutron).

HALF-LIFE:  N = N₀ e^(−λt)      λ = ln2 / t½ ≈ 0.693 / t½
  short t½ ⇒ large λ ⇒ fast decay.  Match t½ + radiation type to the job.
```

### Listing 5 — The binding-energy-per-nucleon curve (landmarks)
```text
Plot: binding energy PER NUCLEON (y) vs mass number A (x).

   BE/nucleon
   (MeV)        ___ iron-56 ≈ 8.8  ← PEAK = most tightly bound, most stable nucleus
      9 |      /‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾———____
        |     /  ⁴He≈7.07          ‾‾‾‾———____ U-235
        |    /                                    (heavy, less bound)
        |  /²H
      0 |_/________________________________________→  mass number A
         light                 56                 heavy

KEY RULE: a reaction releases energy when its products move HIGHER on the curve
  (toward iron) → more tightly bound → mass defect released as E = mc².
   • FUSION: light nuclei (left) combine → climb toward iron → energy released
   • FISSION: heavy nuclei (right) split → climb toward iron → energy released
   • IRON LIMIT: iron-56 is the peak — nothing higher to climb to, so fusing iron or
     beyond ABSORBS energy. ⇒ stellar fusion stops at iron; heavier elements need supernovae.

USEFUL NUMBERS:  1 u ↔ 931.5 MeV  (BE in MeV = mass defect in u × 931.5)
  ⁴He total BE ≈ 28.3 MeV (7.07/nucleon);  iron-56 ≈ 8.8/nucleon;  U-235 fission ≈ 200 MeV.
```

### Listing 6 — Atomic-model development skeleton (evidence → killer → conclusion)
```text
Each model was BUILT by evidence and KILLED by one observation it couldn't explain.
("Can Plum Give Birth, Wonderful" — Cathode, Plum, Gold, Bohr, Waves)

1. CATHODE RAYS → the electron (J.J. Thomson)
   Maltese-cross shadow → travel in straight lines
   paddle wheel turns   → carry momentum / mass
   bend to + plate (E)  → negative charge
   deflect in B field   → charged particles, not light
   ⇒ electron: negative sub-atomic particle. Thomson measured charge-to-mass
     (q/m ≈ 1.76 × 10¹¹ C/kg); Millikan (oil drop) measured the charge alone
     (e = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C), showing charge is quantised.

2. PLUM PUDDING (Thomson): electrons in a sphere of diffuse positive charge.
   KILLED BY → the gold-foil experiment.

3. NUCLEAR ATOM (Rutherford; Geiger–Marsden gold foil)
   most α pass through       → atom mostly empty space
   few α deflected greatly   → positive charge concentrated
   ~1 in 8000 rebound        → tiny, dense, massive nucleus  (α REPELLED, like charges)
   KILLED BY → Maxwell: an orbiting (accelerating) electron must radiate, spiral in
     (<10⁻⁸ s) and give a CONTINUOUS spectrum — but atoms are stable with DISCRETE lines.

4. BOHR: 3 postulates — stationary states (no radiation); photon on a jump
   (hf = E_high − E_low, use the MAGNITUDE); angular momentum quantised.
   Reproduces hydrogen lines; E_n = −13.6/n² eV.
   LIMITS → works only for hydrogen / one-electron ions; can't explain line intensities
     or Zeeman splitting; never explains WHY orbits are quantised.

5. MATTER WAVES (de Broglie λ = h/p; Davisson–Germer & G.P. Thomson electron diffraction)
   ⇒ only orbits whose circumference = a whole number of wavelengths form a standing
     wave → THIS is why orbits are quantised (the mechanism Bohr only asserted).
   (Marks are in the STANDING-WAVE argument, not in quoting λ = h/p.)
   Then Schrödinger (orbitals/probability) + Heisenberg (uncertainty).

UNDERNEATH: nucleons are made of quarks (Standard Model, Listing 3).
```
